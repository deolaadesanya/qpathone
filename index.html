Our Mission

Founded by a pathologist.

"Every diagnosis matters — because behind every slide is a patient waiting for an answer."

Inspired by one family's experience of misdiagnosis, QPathOne was founded by a practising UK Consultant pathologist with a single conviction: access to expert pathology should not depend on where you live.

We provide structured specialist second opinions and support the clinical teams — oncologists, surgeons, and MDT coordinators — who depend on accurate histopathology to make the right decisions for their patients.

The technology exists to serve that mission. Not the other way around.
