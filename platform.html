How it works

From submission to report.

Five steps. No couriers. No delays. Upload your images and clinical details and receive a structured specialist report directly to your portal.

1
Register & add billing contact

Create your hospital account and add your finance contact. Cases cannot be submitted without a billing contact on file.

2
Upload images & clinical details

Upload H&E, IHC, and special stain images by category. Add clinical history, referring clinician, and your specific question.

3
Triage & specialist assignment

QPathOne admin reviews your case within 4 hours, confirms turnaround, and assigns to the appropriate Consultant pathologist.

4
Expert review & structured report

Your Consultant reviews all images and clinical context, then issues a structured second opinion with diagnosis, confidence grading, and recommendations.

5
Delivered & MDT ready

Report delivered to your portal with email notification. Ready for MDT meeting, Tumour board, or treatment planning.
