Our Story

Every diagnosis matters.

"Every diagnosis matters — because behind every slide is a patient waiting for an answer."

QPathOne was inspired by a personal experience of misdiagnosis within our founder's family — a reminder that diagnostic errors are not abstract statistics. They are moments that change lives.

Founded by a UK Consultant pathologist registered with the General Medical Council and US board-certified, QPathOne was built on a simple conviction: expert pathology should be accessible to every patient, regardless of where they live or where they are treated.

We began by offering structured specialist second opinions to clinicians in Nigeria and sub-Saharan Africa — hospitals and oncology centres where access to subspecialty pathology expertise is limited, and where the consequences of diagnostic uncertainty fall heaviest on patients.

We built the digital platform to support that mission — not the other way around. The technology is a tool. The pathologist is the reason to trust.

Mission · Vision · Values
🎯
Mission

To reduce diagnostic errors by making expert pathology more accessible — delivering timely, accurate histopathology second opinions to clinicians and hospitals worldwide.

🌍
Vision

To make expert pathology accessible to every patient, everywhere — so that geography is never a barrier to the highest standard of diagnostic care.

⚖️
Values

Clinical integrity. Transparency. Equity of access. Every decision at QPathOne is guided by what is best for the patient — not by what is easiest for the platform.
