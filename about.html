Our Story

Founded because one family's
experience exposed a problem.

More than 20 years ago, my aunt began what should have been a straightforward medical journey. Instead, an early finding was misdiagnosed as a rare form of leukaemia. As the search for answers continued and treatment was delayed, her condition deteriorated. By the time the correct diagnosis was reached, she had developed gangrene, and her leg ultimately had to be amputated.

"Her story changed the course of my life."

As I pursued a career in pathology, I came to realise that her experience was not an isolated tragedy. Every day, patients around the world suffer because diseases are misdiagnosed, underdiagnosed, or diagnosed too late. For every story that reaches the headlines, there are countless others that never do. For many, particularly in low-resource and underserved communities, access to specialist pathology expertise is limited, and delays in diagnosis can mean the difference between recovery and irreversible harm.

QPathOne was created to be part of the solution.

Our mission is to improve diagnostic accuracy by making high-quality pathology expertise more accessible through standardised digital reporting, intelligent decision support, and modern technology. We believe that every patient deserves a timely, accurate diagnosis, regardless of where they live or the resources available to their healthcare system.

We know that technology alone cannot replace the judgement of experienced pathologists. But by equipping healthcare professionals with better tools, structured workflows, and access to expertise, we believe we can reduce diagnostic errors, support better clinical decisions, and improve patient outcomes.

Our vision is simple: a world where geography is no longer a barrier to accurate diagnosis, and where every pathology report has the potential to change — and save — a life.

Because every diagnosis matters. Every patient matters. Every life matters.
